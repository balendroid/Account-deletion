Plus provides a seamless way to delete your data in-app. In the Circle menu "⋮" navigate to Account management > Delete account

Playstore manages your subscription, by choosing to delete your account future billings will be cancelled

Data that will be deleted

  • Your profile data - interests, phone number, your user-ID, email, and bio. Your contacts will be notified of your account deletion.
  • Created moments - your albums including links to images uploaded on our server. Shared moments to other contacts will persist as copies with you as the author.
  • Created rendezvous - for accepted invites, your name is persisted in the rendezvous details and deleted when every guest in the rendezvous opts to delete their account
  • Your chats and cached data that exist on the device.
